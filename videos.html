VFX: Chilling sheep on a different planet

Description:

The idea of this scene was to create a calm, minimalistic, fantasy environment. Also the sheep in this video is a tribute to small indie game called "Oh, Flock". All 3D models in the video were made in Blender. Grass was made using VFX graph particle strips with a sine wave controlling force direction (sway effect). The fire effect and small particles were also made using VFX graph. Stars in the background were made with a simple shader. Lastly, I added small animation and post processing.


VFX: Holy Battleground v2

Description:

This is a rework of an effect I have made almost a year ago. For this, I have 3D modeled, in Blender, 2 sword models, 2 cross models and some stone debris & pillars. All of this positioned, timed and modified using VFX and shader graph in Unity. The effect was made out of 5 parts: debris on the ground, swords, crosses, God rays and small particles. On top of that post processing and cinemachine with a shaking effect was added to bring this all up together.

VFX: Barrier

Description:

This is a short showcase of a barrier that I made in Unity. The main shape was made in blender. Then it was imported into Unity and with a help of shader graph and Visual Effect graph it received the proper appearance. Lastly, some post processing and simple animation was used to fully showcase it.
